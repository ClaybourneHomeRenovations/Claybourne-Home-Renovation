How It Started

Started over a beer. Built with purpose.

Claybourne Home Renovations didn’t start in a boardroom. It started over a couple of beers between two lifelong friends trying to make sense of a world that was becoming increasingly expensive.

We’ve known each other since elementary school. From school projects and part-time jobs to learning trades and working long hours, we always shared the same mindset: work hard, take pride in what you build, and treat people properly.

Like many homeowners, we kept hearing the same stories: contractors charging luxury prices for average work, families delaying renovations because quality craftsmanship felt out of reach, and homeowners feeling overwhelmed before projects even began.

“Why isn’t there a better option?”

That conversation became Claybourne Home Renovations — a company focused on delivering beautiful, reliable renovations at fair and realistic prices.
