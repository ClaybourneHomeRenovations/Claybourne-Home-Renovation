Before You Reach Out

A little preparation makes the first conversation more useful.

You do not need a finished design or every answer before contacting Claybourne. A few notes about your goals, budget comfort, and concerns help us understand the project faster.

Describe The Space

Tell us which room or area you want to improve, what is not working now, and whether the project is cosmetic, functional, or a larger remodel.

Share Your Priorities

Let us know what matters most: budget, timing, durability, storage, layout, resale value, or simply making the home feel better day to day.

Mention Any Concerns

If you are worried about cost, disruption, material choices, older-home surprises, or where to begin, bring that up early. Those details help shape a better plan.
